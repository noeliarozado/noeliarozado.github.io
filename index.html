🎭 EmocionaApp – Emotional Well-being Through Play

Emociona is a mobile app designed to support emotional self-awareness and regulation through daily emotion tracking and gamified self-care. Developed in Android Studio, it blends psychology-based tools with playful design to encourage consistent use.

  • Emotion Tracker: Log emotions daily, note their causes, and receive tailored self-regulation suggestions.
  • Self-Regulation Tools: Access guided breathing, mindfulness, body scans, and more — all backed by psychological science.
  • PDF Reports: Export emotional logs to share with mental health professionals.
  • Gamified Experience:
    • 🌱 The Enchanted Garden: Grow a plant by logging daily emotions — a fun way to build healthy habits.
    • 🧠 The Maze, Puzzle, Tea Time, and Memory Cards: Mini-games designed to promote focus, calm, and emotional balance.
    • 🏅 Earn monthly badges to stay motivated!

This app bridges emotional intelligence and daily self-care with a fun, educational twist.
